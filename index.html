My name is Zhimin Li(李智闽), I am a CS Ph.D. student from University of Utah. My research interests lie in the area of machine learning, data visualization, and high performance computing. Specifically, I developed novel visualization systems to explain the prediction of machine learning models, and understand the fault tolerance of HPC computation kernels.
