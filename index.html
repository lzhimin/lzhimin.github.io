My name is Zhimin Li(李智闽), I am a CS Ph.D. student from University of Utah. My research interests lie in the area of data visualization and machine learning. Specifically, I developed novel visualization systems to explain the prediction of machine learning models, and perform fault tolerance analysis on HPC computation kernels.
