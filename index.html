Hi, my name is Li (last name), Zhimin (first name). In Chinese, I am called 李智闽. I obtained both my Bachelor's and Ph.D. degrees from the University of Utah, where I was supervised by Valerio Pascucci at the Scientific Computing and Imaging Institute. Currently, I am working with Matthew Berger and Joshua A. Levine as a postdoctoral researcher at Vanderbilt University. My research interests lie in the areas of Visualization, Explainable AI and fault tolerance in large scale computation. [CV].
